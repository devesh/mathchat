# Privacy Policy for Math Tools
Effective Date: March 9, 2026

At Math Tools, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you interact with our Google Chat application.

## 1. Information We Collect
Because Math Tools functions as a utility within Google Chat, we only access the information necessary to provide you with mathematical results:

- Message Content: We receive the text of messages sent directly to @Math Tools or messages in spaces where the app has been mentioned.

- Metadata: We receive basic context from Google, such as your User ID, Space ID, and Display Name, to ensure the response is sent back to the correct person.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data strictly to:

- Analyze and solve the mathematical queries you provide.

- Format and deliver the response back to your Google Chat interface.

- Debug and improve the app’s logic based on error reports.

## 3. Data Retention and Storage
- No Long-Term Storage: We do not maintain a permanent database of your chat history or personal profile.

- Transient Processing: Messages are processed in real-time. Once the calculation is complete and the response is delivered, the message content is typically purged from our active memory.

- Log Data: In the event of a crash, basic technical logs may be generated to help us fix the issue, but these do not include sensitive personal information.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
- Google Infrastructure: As a Google Chat app, your data passes through Google’s servers. Their handling of this data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

- No Sale of Data: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or message content to third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

## 5. Your Rights and Controls
- Removal: You can stop all data collection by removing the Math Tools app from your Google Chat spaces or Direct Messages.

- Access: Since we do not store your data, we generally do not have "records" to provide you upon request, as your data is processed and then discarded.

## 6. Contact and Issue Reporting
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please open a ticket at our official repository:

[https://github.com/devesh/mathchat/issues](https://github.com/devesh/mathchat/issues)

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
